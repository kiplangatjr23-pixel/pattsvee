Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for standard events. For large events (500+ guests), we suggest booking 1-2 months ahead for better menu customization.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us during the booking process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, card payments, and cash. A 30% deposit is required to confirm your booking.

Can you deliver outside Migori?

Yes, we deliver within a 50km radius of Migori. For farther distances, additional transportation fees apply. Contact us for details.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

Cancellations more than 7 days before the event receive a full refund. 3-7 days: 50% refund. Less than 3 days: no refund unless rescheduled.

Do you provide servers and setup?

Yes! All our packages include professional waitstaff, setup, and cleanup. We ensure seamless service throughout your event.
